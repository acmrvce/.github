# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as members and contributors of ACM RVCE, pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards
* Use welcoming and inclusive language.
* Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
* Gracefully accept constructive criticism.
* Focus on what is best for the community.
* Show empathy towards other community members.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the club board or the Webmaster.
